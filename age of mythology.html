Greeks
Civilização padrão.
Villagers generate favor 37,5% faster.
Starts with 10 favor and has a maximum favor of 200 instead of 100.
Hoplites move 12% faster.
Infantry units do 2x damage to buildings.
Stables cost 15% less.
Cavalry units cost 10% less.
Market use costs only 10%.
When Poseidon's buildings are destroyed, Militia emerge from the rubble (the more expensive the structure, the more Militia are created).
Gains a Hippocampus at the Dock if the player also has a Temple. The Hippocampus has no attack, but is a good scout, and will respawn at a dock if killed.
Buildings have 25% more hit points and 20% more attack.
Archers have 10% more attack (including Sentinels).
When human units are killed, there is a 20% chance a Hades Shade will be generated at the Temple (they become more powerful in later ages).


Egyptians
Construções não custam madeira. Algumas contruções são gratuitas. Fazendas podem ser feitas desde a Era Arcaica. Os trabalhadores constroem mais lentamente, mas pode ser acelerado com o Faraó. A Torre de Vigia é evoluída gratuitamente.
Pharaoh empowering yields 25% bonus resources instead of the standard 20%.
Priests can empower drop-off buildings and monuments, but with a 15% resource bonus instead of the Pharaohs' 20%. Priest-empowered buildings are built 30% faster and they research technologies and train units 20% faster.
Camels and Chariot Archers have 22% more hit points and move 10% faster.
Monuments cost 25% less and have 20% more hit points.
Town Centers support +3 maximum Population.
All technologies cost -10% food, wood, and gold.
Monuments have 20% less hit points, but prevent god powers from being targeted nearby.
Priests summon Obelisks 60% faster, Obelisks cost 33% less.
Starts with a Hyena of Set scout.
Priests can convert wild animals.
Pharaoh can summon animals at the cost of favor (the variety of animals the Pharaoh can summon increases with each Age advance).
Five animals are summoned at the Temple on each Age advance.
Slingers and Chariot Archers are trained 20% faster.
Slingers have 10% more HP and 10% less hack vulnerability.
Migdol Strongholds cost 25% less gold.


Norse
Dwarves gather gold 20% faster than Gatherers, but gather food and wood 20% slower. They cost gold instead of food, and train slightly slower than Gatherers.
Gains two Raven Scouts after building a Temple (they reappear if killed).
Hunters gather 20% faster.
Human soldiers constantly regenerate 1,5 HP/s when idle (slower when active).
Huskarls, Portable Rams and Ballistae have 20% more hit points.
Jarls have 15% more hit points.
Starts with two Dwarves instead of two Gatherers.
Dwarves cost 10 less gold, gather food at the same rate as regular Villagers and gather wood only slightly slower.
Builds Dwarven Foundry instead of Armory (is available in the first Age; no Age restriction on technology research; enables three additional technologies).
Dwarven Foundry technologies are cheaper.
Hersirs move 10% faster. Hersirs summon myth units after fighting for long enough. Myth units cost -10% favor. Longhouse units train 10% faster. Ox Carts move 50% faster and cost half as much, but have 45% less HP.


Atlanteans
Citizens ocupam 3 de População. Town Centers geram 0.3 Favor por segundo e são mais baratos mas têm menos pontos de vida e menos ataque. Walls custam 5 gold ao invés de 3.
Human military units move 10% faster.
Shares visibility with allies of ALL Settlements on the map.
Can build Sky Passages (up to 10).
Myth units are 10% cheaper and move 10% faster.
Siege weapons are 10% cheaper and move 10% faster.
Buildings can Time Shift to any location within own Line of Sight.
Economic Guild technologies are 30% cheaper.
Buildings regenerate 3 HP/s.
All buildings are surrounded by lush terrain which cannot be built on by enemies.
Fishing Ships are 10% cheaper and have +25% hit points.
Caravans are 20% cheaper and have +25% hit points.


Informações Ocultas
- Evoluir a Era aumenta os stats (aparência, HP, dano, armadura) das suas unidades atuais e futuras (menos as mitológicas).
