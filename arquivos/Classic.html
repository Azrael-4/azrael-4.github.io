CLASSES
• No Assassin
• No Druid
• No Static Field cap in NM / Hell
• Whirlwind is not affected by any form of Increased attack speed (as a mod or on the weapon base)
• Blessed Hammer receives full %Enhanced Damage from Concentration, instead of the 50% in Expansion
• Summoned creatures, like Necromancer revived monsters, disappear between acts.

ITEMS
• The stash is 6x4 instead of 6x8
• No crafting
• No elite items
• No ethereal items
• Javelins, throwing axes and throwing knives cannot be magic or rare, and cannot be imbued.
• No exceptional or elite uniques, and only the following Amulets and Rings are spawnable:
- Nagelring, Manald Heal, The Stone of Jordan, Nokozan Relic, The Eye of Etlich, The Mahim-Oak Curio.
• No exceptional and elite sets
• No runes (And thus no Runewords)
• No charms
• No circlets
• No class-specific items (Amazon bows, javelins and spears, primal helms, shrunken heads, etc.)

• Affixes in Classic (may have certain errors) http://pastebin.com/raw.php?i=BmmcjjrR
3
GAMEPLAY
• No /players x command
• Blocking does NOT depend on Dexterity, just what is on your Shield + Bonuses
• Mercenaries cannot be resurrected, follow between Acts, receive items/potions, or have their stats viewed

• No Act 5
- And Monsters/Mercenaries that only exist in it, including the Pandemonium Quest
- This removes the level restriction between acts, allowing users to be rushed to Hell with no 'bumper' characters
- This ALSO removes the Larzuk Socket quest from the list of ways you can socket items

• Cube Recipes:
- No making normal quality weapons/armor from low quality ones
- No rerolling magic items with 3x Perfect Gems
- No Tokens of Absolution can be created
- No base items can have sockets added
- No runes can be cubed into higher ones
- No Uniques or Rares can be upgraded
- Items cannot be Repaired or Recharged
- There are no recipes for Pandemonium quest items

• Resistances penalties
-50 in Hell (instead -100)
-20 in Nightmare on Classic (instead -40)
- Also applies to Mana/Stamina damage and reduced poison length

EXTRA
• Diablo Clone does NOT drop an Annihilus Charm, though he will spawn when the correct amount of Stones of Jordan are sold on a server
• Many magic modifiers are missing or changed (Check affix list below, though note that is may have faults)
• Various fairly minor monster stat changes

• Awarded Character titles for completing Difficulties are different:
- For Softcore:
- Defeating Normal is Sir/Dame | Count/Countess
- Defeating Nightmare is Lord/Lady | Duke/Duchess
- Defeating Hell is Baron/Baroness | King/Queen

• Area Levels are different (http://pastebin.com/raw.php?i=SFNvLuGw)
- In Classic, many normal monsters have a hardcored level different from the area level, along with all Super Uniques and Bosses.
