Single Player - CommandsHere's a short list of the most useful commands. The full list (most of which are without function) can be seen below.

-w: Runs Diablo II in windowed mode, at whichever resolution you have specified.
-act1: Characters are level 1 in act 1. (Default setting.)
-act2: Characters are level 16 in act 2.
-act3: Characters are level 21 in act 3.
-act4: Characters are level 27 in act 4.
-act5: Characters are level 33 in act 5.
-nosave: Never saves the game.
-ns: Starts the game without sound.
-skiptobnet: Bypasses the introduction screens by starting the game at the Battle.net login screen.
-seed: Shows the game seed, based on the time of the game creation. Used in some mods and utilities to recreate that exact same game for replay.

Item - CraftCraftar itens é melhor do que re-rollar atributos de um item mágico. Isso porque eles oferecem bônus garantidos MAIS bônus aleatórios, até um máximo de 4 adicionais no ilvl 71.

Para calcular o level do novo item -> ilvl = inteiro(clvl * 0.5) + inteiro(ilvl * 0.5)

Assim, é melhor craftar vários amuletos do Caster do que re-rolar os afixos de um amuleto mágico.

Item - OskillsOskills are capped to +3 skill levels if used by their native class. For example, the four-piece set bonus for Trang-Oul's Avatar is +10 to Meteor. Classes other than Sorceress will be able to cast a level 10 Meteor (or higher, with + to all skills) while wearing four pieces of Trang-Oul's. A Sorceress wearing the same will receive only +3 to her existing Meteor skill, however. This allows oskill bonuses to be large enough to be useful to other classes without being overpowered for their native class.
