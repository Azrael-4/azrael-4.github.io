Ascension
1There will be approximately 60% more Elites spawned in a map.
2Normal enemies deal more damage with their attacks.
3Elites deal more damage with their attacks.
4Bosses deal more damage with their attacks.
5Heal for 75% of missing health instead of 100%.
6Lose 10% health at the start of each run.
7Normal enemies have more HP. Some gain higher Icon Block Block. They are overall harder to take down.
8Elites have more HP.
9Bosses have more HP. Some gain higher Block. They are overall harder to take down.
10Obtain 1 Ascender's Bane (Curse: Unplayable, Etheral) at the start of each run.
11Start each run with 1 less potion slot.
12Upgraded cards are 50% less likely to appear in Act 2 (12.5%, down from 25%) and Act 3 (25%, down from 50%).
13Bosses drop 25% less gold
14Max HP: -5 for Ironclad, -4 for Silent, Defect, and Watcher
15Many events have less positive outcomes and more severe consequences. For example: More HP loss. Less gold or card as rewards. Guaranteed Curse.
16Shops cost 10% more.
17Normal enemies have more challenging movesets and abilities.
18Elites have more challenging movesets and abilities.
19Bosses have more challenging movesets and abilities.
20After you defeat the first boss that can be seen from the map, you will immediately enter a battle with a different Act 3 boss.

ElitesThe chances for relics from an elite fight are 50% for a common, 33% for an uncommon and 17% for a rare.
